We help local governments use data to solve critical problems

Statecraft is a San Francisco-based urban analytics company. We partner with cities, counties, and states to address biggest challenges faced by their communities.

Statecraft helps measure, predict, and improve key indicators in housing affordability, job creation, and tax revenue. Our technology solutions cover data integration (merging multiple sources into a single integrated database), advanced analysis (predictions, alerts, optimizations), and interface design (dashboards, reports, visualizations, maps).

Using components from Statecraft platform, new initiatives can be launched in weeks, not months.
